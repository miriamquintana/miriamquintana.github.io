Weddings & Events

The Meadow Package – $750

Perfect for intimate weddings, this package includes a bridal bouquet, a matching boutonnière, and small centerpieces for up to six tables. Designed with seasonal, locally grown flowers, the Meadow Package is simple, elegant, and eco-friendly. Ideal for couples who want a beautiful touch without overwhelming their budget or their venue.

Our most popular option, the Garden Package includes a bridal bouquet, two bridesmaid bouquets, boutonnieres for the wedding party, floral accents for the ceremony space, and centerpieces for up to ten tables. This package focuses on lush greenery and fresh blooms, combining sustainability with romance. Each arrangement is custom-tailored to your wedding’s color palette.

Designed in close collaboration with our florists, this package ensures your event is unforgettable.
